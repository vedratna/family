# Settings & Customization

## Theme Colors

Each family can have its own accent color. The Owner or Admin selects from 8 options:

| Theme          | Color            | Feeling               |
| -------------- | ---------------- | --------------------- |
| Teal (default) | Calm green-blue  | Trustworthy, grounded |
| Indigo         | Deep purple-blue | Modern, clean         |
| Coral          | Warm orange-red  | Warm, inviting        |
| Sage           | Muted green      | Earthy, natural       |
| Amber          | Golden yellow    | Warm, energetic       |
| Ocean          | Medium blue      | Fresh, open           |
| Plum           | Purple-pink      | Rich, distinctive     |
| Slate          | Cool gray        | Minimal, neutral      |

**Tip:** If you're in multiple families, different theme colors help you instantly recognize which family you're viewing.

To change: **More > Family Settings > Family Theme**

## Dark Mode

Choose your preferred appearance:

- **Light** — warm white backgrounds
- **Dark** — warm dark backgrounds (easier on eyes at night)
- **System** (default) — follows your phone's system setting

Dark mode uses warm dark tones (#1A1A1A), never pure black, for a softer feel.

To change: **More > Dark Mode**

## Notification Preferences

Control what notifications you receive for each family:

| Category               | Default | What It Covers                                     |
| ---------------------- | ------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| Events & Reminders     | **ON**  | Birthday reminders, upcoming events, event changes |
| Comments on your posts | **ON**  | When someone comments on your posts                |
| Social Feed            | OFF     | New posts from family members                      |
| Family Updates         | OFF     | New member joined, role changes                    |

To change: **More > Notification Settings**

## Managing Family Members

**Admins and Owners can:**

- Invite new members (by phone number)
- Change member roles (Admin, Editor, Viewer)
- Remove members (their Person record stays in the tree)
- Add non-app members to the tree

**Owners can additionally:**

- Transfer ownership to another member
- Delete the family

To manage: **More > Family Settings > Members**

## Transferring Ownership

If you're the Owner and want to pass control:

1. Go to **Family Settings > Members**
2. Tap the member you want to make Owner
3. Tap **Transfer Ownership**
4. Confirm — you'll become an Admin, and they become the new Owner
