# Home Feed

> The home screen of FamilyApp — where your family shares moments.

## What You'll See

The feed shows a timeline of posts from your family members, newest first. Between posts, you'll also see **upcoming event cards** for events within the next 7 days (like "Grandma's Birthday — 3 days away!").

## Creating Posts

Tap the post composer at the top of the feed. You can:

- Write **text** updates
- Attach **photos** from your gallery or camera
- Attach **videos** (MP4, MOV — up to 500MB)
- Combine text + media in one post

**Who can post?** Editors, Admins, and Owners. Viewers can read and react but not create posts.

## Reacting to Posts

Tap the heart icon on any post to react. Quick reactions available: ❤️ 👍 😂 😍 😢 🙏

Your reaction is highlighted so you can see which posts you've reacted to. Tap again to remove your reaction.

## Commenting

Tap the comment icon to open the full comment view. Type your comment and tap Send. Comments appear in chronological order.

## Sharing Externally

Tap the **Share** button on any post to open your phone's native share sheet. From there you can send to WhatsApp, Instagram, or any other app.

## Activation Gate

**If you just created your family and no one has joined yet**, posting is temporarily locked. This is by design — your first posts should have an audience! While waiting, you can:

- Add events to the calendar
- Add family members to the tree
- Resend invites

Once the first member joins, posting unlocks automatically.
